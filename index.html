Who We Are

Founded originally as an IT firm specializing in products with technological advancements in 2013, the company has made steady inroad into the growing market of Papua New Guinea through reliable product delivery and aftersales services. With the company’s extensive knowledge in import-export system and direct connections with overseas manufacturers and large-scale suppliers, Larkin Enterprises is able to compete with the leading market players in PNG, promoting Great Price, Better Quality.

As Papua New Guinea’s economy and popularity grows across the Pacific and neighbouring countries and with the sudden boost in the Building and Construction Industry, the need for innovative products also increased... and so the company took its chance in expanding its market through introduction of high quality, state-of-the-art products such as IT equipment, air-conditioning systems, tracking and security devices, noise reduction products and building materials.

What We Do

We are steadily creating our mark as an organization of well-experienced suppliers-importers of state-of-the-art products like building materials, air conditioning systems, IT equipments, alarm and tracking devices with un-paralleled dedication to quality.

The company’s commitment to promoting innovations in the construction industry by providing contractors and businesses alike access to better quality products, together with the dedication to excellence in customer service, we aim to be the top supplier company here in Papua New Guinea while pursuing to be a solid foundation that supports its people and the economy.
